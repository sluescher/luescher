Hi! I am Stephanie Luescher. I just finished my PhD at Princeton, where I spent years studying what people wrote in the margins of medieval Arabic manuscripts. At Princeton, I served as graduate coordinator for MARBAS and organized its flagship event series PUL & MARBAS present..." I co-organized Comparative Diplomatics, a workshop exploring documents across late antiquity and the middle ages. I was also a Data Fellow at the Center for Digital Humanities and a University Administrative Fellow at the Program for Community Engaged Scholarship.
